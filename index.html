Who We Are

A Force for
Positive Change

UNITED SIKHS is an international, non-profit humanitarian organization affiliated with the United Nations. Rooted in the Sikh values of seva (selfless service), equality, and compassion, we are committed to empowering disadvantaged and minority communities across the globe.

Our mission transcends boundaries of religion, race, and nationality. We provide support to those in need, advocate for justice, and nurture communities through education and empowerment.

UNITED SIKHS is an international, non-profit humanitarian organization affiliated with the United Nations. Founded in 1999 and rooted in Sikh values of seva (selfless service), we empower disadvantaged communities through humanitarian aid, civil rights advocacy, and community education across 30+ countries.
Donations directly fund our three core programs: Sikh Aid (disaster relief and food distribution), ICHRA (civil rights and legal aid), and CEED (education and community empowerment). We maintain full transparency with annual reports available on our website.
Visit unitedsikhs.org/join-us to sign up. We have chapters worldwide and welcome volunteers of all backgrounds for disaster relief, community events, and advocacy campaigns.
We operate in over 30 countries across 6 continents, with headquarters in New York. Our chapters span North America, Europe, Asia, Africa, and Australia.
Contact our ICHRA team through the website or call +1-855-US-UMEED (+1-855-878-6333). We provide free legal aid and support for victims of hate crimes and discrimination.
